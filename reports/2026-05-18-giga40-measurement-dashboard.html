🚦 Checkpoint T7 — Mid-Awareness Review

Khi nào: Cuối tuần 2 tháng 7 (sau ~2 tuần Awareness phase). Mục đích: validate awareness trajectory, adjust media spend, kill content tệ.

Checklist 6 items

  1. ✅ Total reach đạt ≥ 30% target (=2.4M+)? — nếu KHÔNG → boost paid ads spend +20%
  2. ✅ KOL Tier 2 wave drops xong? — nếu KHÔNG → escalate KOL agency, có backup KOL?
  3. ✅ Mid Lucky #1 broadcast xong? Engagement spike confirmed? — nếu KHÔNG → adjust Mid #2 prize tier
  4. ✅ Top 3 best-performing content piece là gì? — double-down vào content style đó cho phase 4
  5. ✅ Bottom 3 worst-performing content? — KILL hoặc pivot
  6. ✅ Lucky Draw entries ≥ 5K? — nếu KHÔNG → ngộ ra issue ở promotion mechanic (QR not scanning? landing slow?)

Output deliverable

  • Mid-T7 report HTML — em sẽ auto-gen qua MCP stack mỗi cuối tuần 2 T7
  • Decision memo — 1 trang, 3 actions cho phase 4
  • Budget reallocation đề xuất (nếu cần)

🚦 Checkpoint T8 — Pre-Climax Review

Khi nào: Cuối tuần 2 tháng 8 (sau ~6 tuần campaign). Mục đích: lock readiness cho Climax phase, confirm Festival logistic, finalize Grand Prize.

Checklist 8 items

  1. ✅ Total reach ≥ 60% target (=4.8M+)? — pacing healthy?
  2. ✅ Festival registration ≥ 70% (=1,400+)? — nếu < 50% → escalate KOL + paid push reg
  3. ✅ University workshop completed all 4 trường? — nếu KHÔNG → schedule shift
  4. ✅ Mid Lucky #2 drawn, engagement confirmed?
  5. ✅ POSM Moment #2 (đếm ngược) deployed 39 retailer?
  6. ✅ Festival venue locked + production vendor confirmed?
  7. ✅ Grand Prize finalized (AORUS top-tier RTX 5090 setup ~150tr)?
  8. ✅ Sale uplift trajectory cho +35% target khả thi? — nếu < +20% → emergency: tăng promo depth + flash sale tần suất

Output deliverable

  • Mid-T8 report HTML
  • Festival GO / NO-GO decision — risk assessment
  • Final phase 5 brief cho team execution

🌳 Decision Tree — Nếu metric X off, làm gì?

🔴 IF Reach < 50% target sau T7

→ Diagnose: ad spend OK? Creative fatigue? Channel mix sai? Action: refresh 2 hero creative + tăng paid 25% + thử new channel (Threads/Discord?)

🔴 IF Festival registration < 50% sau T8

→ Đe doạ KPI flagship. Action: KOL drive sign-up (Tier 1 livestream), giveaway code "Đăng ký nhận quà tham dự", paid retarget warm audience.

🔴 IF Sale uplift < +15% sau T7

→ Promotion mechanic chưa work. Action: deep dive — promo depth không đủ? SKU không hot? Stock issue? Pricing competitive review vs CellphoneS.

🟡 IF University workshop < 50% sau T8 W2

(Hiện tại đang ở case này). Action: shift schedule, thêm extra perk (free demo unit / pizza), partner club esports trường để pull crowd.

🟡 IF UGC submission < 30% pace

→ Challenge không attractive. Action: add weekly mini-prize, seed KOL Tier 3 chạy first 50 submission, simplify entry mechanic.

🟢 IF mọi KPI ≥ 90% target

→ Action: keep status quo, không thay đổi. Maintain content quality, đừng over-spend. Reallocate buffer 5% sang Climax phase để đầu tư Festival production.
